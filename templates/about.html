Lagos Rent Estimator

Welcome to the Lagos Rent Estimator, your go-to tool for predicting rental prices in Lagos, Nigeria based on key property features. A platform designed to help individuals, property managers, and real estate professionals gain insights into current market trends and rental values across various neighborhoods in Lagos.

How It Works

The Lagos Rent Estimator leverages a machine learning model, specifically a Decision Tree Regressor, trained on historical rent data across Lagos, achieving an accuracy score of over 85%. This indicates a high level of success in predicting rental prices, as the model can accurately estimate rents 85% of the time, making it a reliable tool for providing rent predictions in the Lagos area. By inputting details such as property type, location, number of bedrooms, and number of bathrooms, The Model predicts a reasonable rental estimate based on these parameters.

This approach allows for more accurate rent predictions, giving users an edge when negotiating or evaluating property prices. The Model predictions consider various factors that influence rent prices in Lagos, ensuring that you are equipped with the most relevant information.

Why Choose Lagos Rent Estimator?

Whether you are a tenant, a landlord, or a real estate agent, having access to reliable rent price estimates can help you make better decisions. This platform offers:

  • Accurate predictions based on data-driven models.
  • Instant results with an easy-to-use interface.
  • Coverage of key neighborhoods across Lagos, including Lekki, Ajah, Alimosho, Gbagada, and more.

Mission

My mission is to bring transparency and ease to the Lagos real estate market by providing a powerful tool that empowers users to make informed decisions. I am dedicated to refining The Model and expanding coverage to deliver the most accurate predictions possible.
